Privacy Policy – ShadowChat

Last updated: May 2026

ShadowChat respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how ShadowChat collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Profile information you choose to provide

2. Messages and Media

ShadowChat allows users to send:

  • Text messages
  • Images
  • Voice messages

Messages are protected using secure encryption methods.

3. Microphone Permission

ShadowChat requests microphone access only for recording and sending voice messages. The microphone is never used without user action.

4. Camera & Storage Permissions

ShadowChat may request access to:

  • Camera
  • Photos and media files

These permissions are used only for messaging features.

5. How We Use Information

  • Provide messaging functionality
  • Improve app performance and security
  • Maintain account functionality
  • Prevent abuse and unauthorized access

6. Third-Party Services

ShadowChat uses services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
